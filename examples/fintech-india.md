## COMMON BLIND SPOTS

- **Bharat dynamics:** Most fintech councils analyze metro/urban patterns and miss rural reality
- **Vernacular UX:** English-first product design misses 90% of India's TAM
- **Cash dependency:** Even digital products operate in cash-adjacent economy
- **Trust dynamics:** Brand trust takes 5+ years; fintech often plans on 2-year horizon
- **Regulatory whiplash:** RBI position can change overnight (e.g., crypto, foreign NBFCs)
