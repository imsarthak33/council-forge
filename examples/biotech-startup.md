## COMMON BLIND SPOTS IN BIOTECH COUNCILS

- **Payer dynamics:** Coverage and reimbursement often analyzed too late
- **Patient advocacy:** Patient organizations as commercial accelerators undervalued
- **Geographic strategy:** Ex-US (EMA, PMDA, NMPA) often afterthought
- **IP timing:** Patent runway often miscalculated relative to development timelines
- **Talent retention:** Scientist attrition during preclinical-to-clinical transition
