## COMMON BLIND SPOTS IN CLIMATE TECH COUNCILS

- **Workforce reality:** Energy transition needs ~30M jobs trained; rarely analyzed in strategy
- **Critical minerals:** Lithium, copper, rare earth supply chain constraints
- **Adaptation vs mitigation:** Most councils focus on mitigation; adaptation underweighted
- **Climate justice:** Global South perspectives often missing from Western-dominated panels
- **Behavioral change:** Demand-side reductions consistently underestimated
- **Geopolitical risk:** China-US-EU climate cooperation/competition dynamics
